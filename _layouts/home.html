Lab Mission and Research Directions

The Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Laboratory is dedicated to advancing cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics technologies. We focus on developing innovative algorithms and systems to solve complex real-world problems and nurture the next generation of leaders in AI and robotics.

Research Methods and Approaches

We adopt interdisciplinary research methods, combining technologies such as deep learning, reinforcement learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. The laboratory is equipped with advanced robotic platforms and high-performance computing resources, providing strong technical support for research work.
